Occupation and education

For the past several years, my professional career has revolved around machining, welding various types of metals, as well as constructing steel and aluminum structures, and engineering. I began my career as an assistant blacksmith, and currently, I utilize technical documentation to fabricate and weld steel devices used in the medical industry. Additionally, I have completed engineering studies at the Faculty of Mechanical Engineering, specializing in programming CNC machine tools and devices. During my time at the University of Technology in Koszalin, I had the opportunity to work with CAD and CAM software, which supported the design and manufacturing process of objects using CNC devices. One of my most memorable experiences was working with the Solidworks program. Currently, I have completed the Frontend Developer from Scratch course as I am deeply interested in pursuing a career in IT.

Goals and dreams

For approximately 7 years, I have been working at the same company, where I am involved in the production of equipment for the medical industry. Despite completing courses in "coded welding" and "responsible welding coordination" a few years ago, I do not see significant opportunities for further development in this field. In my quest for new opportunities, I have developed a strong interest in the IT industry due to its promising growth prospects, remote work possibilities, competitive salaries, and health benefits. I have recently completed a frontend developer course and discovered a profound sense of satisfaction and motivation in programming, fueling my desire for continuous improvement. Thanks to the frontend developer course, I have mastered languages such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, the React library, and the distributed version control system GIT. At this point, I am highly motivated to transition and embark on my first job in the IT field. My plan is to continually expand my knowledge in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and related areas, all while enriching my portfolio to the fullest extent. Ultimately, my dream is to secure a position as a junior developer, paving the way for a new and fulfilling career.
